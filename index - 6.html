TITLE SUMMARY RATINGS
Elemental "Elemental" is a fantasy novel following a young protagonist named Callum Hunt, who discovers his magical abilities and is thrust into a world of intrigue and danger. As he navigates through a magical school, Callum uncovers dark secrets about his past and the true nature of magic itself.
Mulan "Mulan," a young Chinese woman disguises herself as a man to take her father's place in the army, fighting against invading forces. Through bravery, skill, and determination, she ultimately saves China and earns respect for herself and her family.
The Little Mermaid "The Little Mermaid" tells the story of Ariel, a mermaid princess who dreams of becoming human and experiencing life on land. She makes a deal with the sea witch Ursula to trade her voice for legs, but ultimately learns the value of her true self and love.
